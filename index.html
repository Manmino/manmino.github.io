Manmino

Auxiliary Language of Asia

About Manmino

Manmino is a constructed East Asian Auxiliary language, designed to be spoken across East Asia instead of English to make intra-regional communication easier. Team Manmino hopes through the spread of this language, people of East Asia would be able to understand each other better, allowing for more intra-regional cooperation in pursuit of intellectual and artistic achievement.
